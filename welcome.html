Welcome!

When I decided that I wanted to compile all of my experiences into this blog, my first instinct was to go back and take a look at all the journals and blog posts that I kept while I was abroad. To take a look at the first trips that inspired my photography and tell the stories. My older stories are a little further back, they are admittedly a little more disjointed but I did my best to edit it from memory and photos. This journal is meant to share my experiences with my friends and family but also meant mostly for myself. I want to keep track of everything amazing that I've been able to experience and learn about as I explore the world and be able to look back as I believe that every trip inspires growth and everyone to see with their eyes a little wider. I hope you enjoy :)
